Deftones, originally from California, blend alternative metal, rock, and shoegaze, with their career peak marked by the album "White Pony" (2000).

The Strokes are an alternative rock band from New York, renowned for their distinctive sound and the iconic album "Is This It" (2001).

Pixies

Pixies are an alternative rock band hailing from Boston, pioneers of the genre known for their dark lyrics and dynamic songs, especially on the album "Doolittle" (1989).
